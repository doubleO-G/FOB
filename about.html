Our Story

How Faith Over Brokenness Was Born

Faith Over Brokenness was birthed out of a personal journey with God—a walk through seasons of pain, disappointment, and unanswered questions. What looked like an ending became a new beginning when the Lord revealed that even in brokenness, His purpose still stands.

As I leaned on Christ, He showed me that faith is not just believing for blessings, but trusting Him in the fire, under the broom tree, and on the mountain top. Out of that revelation, this ministry was born—not to magnify brokenness, but to proclaim the victory of faith in Jesus Christ.

"This is the victory that has overcome the world—even our faith." — 1 John 5:4

Faith Over Brokenness Ministries exists to heal the wounded, restore the weary, disciple believers, and release them into their God-given destiny. Just as God turned my ashes into beauty, this ministry carries the testimony that He can do the same for every life that yields to Him.

Vision & Mission

Our calling and purpose in Christ

Vision

"To raise a generation that is healed, restored, discipled, and released to walk in their God-given purpose, bearing testimony of Christ."

Mission

"Through the Word, Spirit, and Christ-centered discipleship, Faith Over Brokenness Ministries journeys with the broken, lifting them from despair to restoration, discipleship, and release into their eternal purpose in Christ."

Core Objectives

How we serve the body of Christ

Evangelism & Outreach

Share the message of salvation through Christ locally and globally using digital platforms, missions, and community outreach.

Discipleship & Teaching

Establish structured discipleship programs rooted in Scripture to help believers grow in faith, obedience, and love.

Restoration & Healing

Minister to the brokenhearted, marginalized, and wounded by offering prayer, counseling, and Christ-centered support.

Empowerment & Equipping

Train believers to discover and operate in their God-given gifts and callings for service in the Kingdom.

Worship & Prayer

Cultivate a lifestyle of intimacy with God through worship gatherings, intercession, and teaching on the secret place.

Community & Fellowship

Build strong Christ-centered communities that foster unity, accountability, and love among believers.
