Prayer & Worship

Drawing near to God in prayer and worship

At the heart of Faith Over Brokenness Ministries is a deep desire to draw near to God in prayer and worship. We believe prayer is not just a request line but a lifeline—our constant communion with the Father. Worship is more than a song; it is the posture of our hearts, surrendering to the One who restores and sustains us.

Here, we cultivate the secret place, walk under the shadow of the Almighty, and dwell in the still waters of His presence.

"He leads me beside quiet waters, He restores my soul." — Psalm 23:2-3

Scriptural Foundation

"God is spirit, and His worshipers must worship in the Spirit and in truth."
