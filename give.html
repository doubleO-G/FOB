Building God's Kingdom together

At Faith Over Brokenness Ministries, we believe that giving is an act of worship and faith. Every seed sown helps us proclaim Christ, disciple believers, and bring healing to the brokenhearted. Through your generosity, lives are restored, families are strengthened, and the gospel of Jesus Christ reaches farther.

Together, we are building a legacy of faith and victory.
