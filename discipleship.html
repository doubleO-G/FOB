Discipleship Journey

Growing deeper in Christ and walking in victory

At Faith Over Brokenness Ministries, we believe that true victory in life comes only through faith in Jesus Christ. Discipleship is at the heart of our calling—it is the journey of growing deeper in Christ, learning His Word, and living out the Great Commission.

Our discipleship program, Faith That Overcomes, is designed to equip believers to overcome life's challenges and walk in their God-given identity as victorious sons and daughters of the Kingdom.

"For everyone born of God overcomes the world. This is the victory that has overcome the world—even our faith." — 1 John 5:4

Our Discipleship Journey

Five essential stages of spiritual growth

1

Foundations of Faith

Understanding salvation and the new life in Christ. Identity and assurance as children of God. Walking in obedience to the Word.

2

Growing in the Word

How to study and apply Scripture daily. The role of the Holy Spirit in revelation and guidance. Living a lifestyle of prayer and intimacy with God.

3

Healing and Restoration

Overcoming past wounds, failures, and bondages. Discovering freedom in Christ through forgiveness and surrender. Embracing the wholeness God promises in His presence.

4

Walking in Victory

Developing unshakable faith in trials. Cultivating a victorious mindset rooted in God's promises. Testifying of Christ's victory in everyday life.

5

Equipped for Service

Discovering spiritual gifts and callings. Serving God with love and humility. Becoming a disciple-maker: leading others to faith and growth.
