Welcome to Faith Over Brokenness Ministries

We are a Christ-centered ministry called to bring healing, hope, and restoration to the brokenhearted.

We stand today as living testimonies that faith in Jesus truly overcomes every season of life. Though we have walked through valleys of disappointment and faced the weight of brokenness, God's love never let us go. He lifted us, restored our hope, and taught us that faith is not about avoiding trials, but about trusting Him through them.

"This is the victory that has overcome the world—even our faith." — 1 John 5:4

Our stories are not about what we lost, but about the One we gained—Christ, our strength and our victory.

Our Founder

Meet Priscar Werunga, the founder of Faith Over Brokenness Ministries

Priscar Werunga - Founder

I am a follower of Christ and the founder of Faith Over Brokenness Ministries. My journey has been marked by trials, but God's grace has turned every test into a testimony. Through His love, I discovered that brokenness is not the end—it's the place where faith rises.

My calling is to encourage, heal, restore, and disciple others, releasing them into their God-given purpose. I share the message of hope that in Christ we overcome, and that no season of pain is wasted when placed in His hands.

Faith Over Brokenness is not just my story—it's an invitation for others to experience the same transforming power of Jesus Christ.

Our Ministries

We serve through various ministries to bring healing and restoration

Evangelism & Outreach

Sharing the message of salvation through Christ locally and globally using digital platforms, missions, and community outreach.

Discipleship & Teaching

Establishing structured discipleship programs rooted in Scripture to help believers grow in faith, obedience, and love.

Restoration & Healing

Ministering to the brokenhearted, marginalized, and wounded by offering prayer, counseling, and Christ-centered support.

Empowerment & Equipping

Training believers to discover and operate in their God-given gifts and callings for service in the Kingdom.

Worship & Prayer

Cultivating a lifestyle of intimacy with God through worship gatherings, intercession, and teaching on the secret place.

Community & Fellowship

Building strong Christ-centered communities that foster unity, accountability, and love among believers.
